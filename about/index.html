About Hanzu Tech

technically

Designer

AKA Jack of All Trades

Hanzu Tech is a focused product studio for useful systems. The background is practical: design, fabrication thinking, technical build work, operations, and direct problem-solving for jewelry businesses that need more leverage and less noise. The philosophy is simple: one person should be able to carry an idea from sketch, to interface, to workflow, to shipped object without losing the plot.

Background

The work lives at the intersection of technical design, software tooling, and physical product thinking. That means quoting systems, jewelry production helpers, admin interfaces, and whatever else closes the gap between an idea and an operational result. The end state is independence through useful tech, not trend-chasing.
