FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is Hanzu Tech building? 01
Technically designed products, internal tools, and production-aware systems. The first wave is focused on jewelry operations, but the broader mission is any useful tool that solves real workflow pain and can be shipped responsibly.
Why focus on jewelry first? 02
Because the bottlenecks are obvious: quoting, design translation, admin cleanup, manufacturing handoff, and repetitive manual work. That makes the value of better tools visible very fast.
Where does AI actually help here? 03
AI speeds research, reference discovery, workflow automation, internal agents, and structured outputs. It is leverage, not decoration. The system still needs technical taste and production knowledge to stay useful.
Is this design only or full product execution? 04
Both. Hanzu Tech spans visual direction, 3D work, CAD logic, factory-aware process design, AI pipelines, and software tools where needed. The objective is execution, not isolated deliverables.
